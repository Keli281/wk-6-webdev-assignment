Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility requirements? +

Applicants must be Kenyan citizens aged 18-35 with a high school diploma. Basic computer skills are recommended but not required.

How long is the programme? +

The programme runs for 6 months, including 3 months of intensive training and 3 months of internship placement.

Is there any cost involved? +

The programme is fully funded for selected candidates through a partnership with the Kenyan Ministry of Education and technology partners.
